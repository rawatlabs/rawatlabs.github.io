Navigating the Outer World (Maya)

"Maya" is the world of appearances. The AGI age will amplify distractions. Thriving requires wise engagement.

Principle 1: Create More Than You Consume

Resist the pull of passive consumption. Instead of endless scrolling, dedicate time to an active craft: write, code, build, garden. Creation is the antidote to emptiness.
